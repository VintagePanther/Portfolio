The Art of Logos:
Crafting Identity through Graphic Design



Tools Used :

Procreate - Illustrator - Wacom

Latest:

Year 2023


In each new project, meticulous attention is devoted to the selection of symbols, text, colors, and shapes. Colors invoke emotions and perceptions, while typography establishes the brand's tone and personality.

As a result, whether crafting a logo or orchestrating a complete rebranding, my work unfolds in four vital phases:

1. Research and Discovery: Immersion in the brand's essence and values.
2. Conceptualization and Sketching: Shaping ideas and visual concepts.
3. Digital Design and Refinement: Transforming concepts into polished digital designs.
4. Feedback and Iterations: Fine-tuning the design based on valuable input.
